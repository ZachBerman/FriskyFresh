Our Story


Frisky Fresh Fish was started by Sean Brownell in Whitehorse Yukon Canada. After a successful couple of years of bringing the best Alaskan Seafood to the Yukon, Sean and I set our sights on Utah. After many years of struggling to find a good source of seafood, I decided to open a Frisky Fresh Fish branch here Salt Lake City. The product that we get from the fisherman of Alaska is unrivaled in taste, quality, freshness or texture. I've confirmed this in my travels between Haines and Salt Lake. Every time I bought seafood from a local store here in Utah, I was always disappointed. There was something missing and it was the high quality of the seafood that comes from Alaska. Here at Frisky Fresh Fish we sell only the best wild and sustainably caught seafood. One thing that is extremely important to us at Frisky Fresh Fish is that our product in caught in a responsible way so that future generations can enjoy this great product from the cold clean waters of Alaska.
